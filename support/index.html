# Support for RAADGAMES

Welcome to the official support page for Drift Masters! We're here to help with any issues, answer your questions, or take your feedback to improve the game.

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. **How do I reset my progress?**

Go to Settings > Account > Reset Progress.

2. **What should I do if the app crashes?**

Ensure you are using the latest version of the app. If the problem persists, contact us.
