Frequently Asked Questions

1. What should I do if the app crashes?

• Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed from the App Store or Google Play.

• Restart your device and try reopening the app.

• If the issue persists, contact us using the details below.

2. How can I report a bug or problem?

Send us an email with the following information:

  • A description of the issue.
  • Your device model and operating system version.
  • The app version you’re using.
